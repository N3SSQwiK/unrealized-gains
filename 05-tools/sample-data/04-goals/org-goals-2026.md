## Goal 1: Modernize the Control Inventory Framework
Redesign the control inventory to align with updated regulatory expectations and reduce manual overhead. Deliver a proposal by Q2 and begin implementation in Q3.

## Goal 2: Formalize AI Risk Framework
Establish a formal AI risk assessment framework for wealth management, including model risk classification, usage policies, and monitoring procedures.

## Goal 3: Reduce Operational Loss Events by 20%
Through improved controls, better training, and proactive risk identification, reduce operational loss events by 20% year-over-year.

## Goal 4: Develop Team Bench Strength
Ensure each direct report has a documented development plan and at least one stretch assignment in 2026. Build succession readiness.
